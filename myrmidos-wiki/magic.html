Myrmidos Wiki: Magic

Overview

Magic is a powerful and volatile practice that involves invoking energy from another plane of existence to produce a desired effect. There are three basic types of magic, each differing in the point of origin for the magic itself and the nature of casting required. Arcane Magic is achieved through study, and involves plucking on the strands of another plane that are already present in the world. Divine Magic is granted by another being, with the caster serving as an intermediary to harness and direct the magical energy. Innate Magic involves a being who has a physiological connection to the other Planescapes invoking that connection, much like a muscle, and requires the least academic knowledge to cast.
