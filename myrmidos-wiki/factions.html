Myrmidos Wiki: Factions

Overview

The links to the right contain lists with all factions of that type present. The Factions in Myrmidos include any group of individuals who coordinate towards a common goal. Some specific factions can be found under multiple categories. Governing Nations are those who have actively held power at some point. Political Contingents include any group who has a political agenda included in their common goal. Religious Sects include those who worship any of the Greater Deities and act in accordance with their principles. Occult Followings include those who worship indifferent beings such as the Titans or Genies. Commercial Guilds include unions and similar associations for merchants, tradesmen, and craftsmen. Criminal Guilds are exactly that, guilds dedicated to gaining power or coin through crime. Most have political agendas to fund, and are usually at odds with the local government. Terrorist Groups are usually Occults Followings or Political Contingents, but can be any type of Faction who resorts to violence against the general populace in order to achieve their goals. These groups are often considered the common enemy of all other Factions in Myrmidos.

The Champions of Frydeia The Magistrate Members are any Lawful alignment Exists outside of all nations Seeks to aid in maintaining order with and between nations. Utilizes diplomacy and negotiation, resorting to violence only when pressed. Members include gishes, psions, and paladins. The Champions of Deliklese The Shamans Members are any Good alignment Exists within mutliple nations Seeks to aid in preserving natural life by destroying monsters. Utilizes knowledge, martial prowess, and divine magic to destroy that which is evil. Members include Druids, Rangers, Clerics, and Blood Hunters. The Champions of Gondarin The Artisans Members are any alignment Exists within every nation Seeks to promote art and expression, including violence such as war. Utilizes skill for crafting, writing, and any other creative activity. Members include Bards, Artificers, Fighters, and even Rogues. The Champions of Sebabos The Shades Members are any Evil alignment. Exists within several nations secretly. Seeks to observe death directly, both through murdering others intimately and dying themselves. Utilizes any measure to inflict death and suffering. Members include Rogues, Barbarians, Clerics, and Wizards.
