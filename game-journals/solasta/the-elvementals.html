These are the recordings of my Solasta gameplay in a new campaign. The party is called the Elvementals as they are all Half-Elves and each take after a different variety of Genasi.

The face of the group is Ignis, a Spy Oath of Tirmar Paladin. She is a holy warrior who can channel divine energy into her attacks. The support of the group is Aqua, an Academic Battle Cleric. He is a war priest who wields divine magic. The thief of the group is Terra, a Lowlife Shadow-Tamer Ranger. She is a huntress with lockpicking skills and nature magic. The mage of the group is Caeli, a Sellsword Shock Arcanist Wizard. He wields arcane magic and keeps his knowledge in a spellbook.
