The World of Nraanon

A comprehensive Wiki built to showcase my HTML skills and document my fictional world


The world of Nraanon takes heavy inspiration from a specific time period in the Forgotten Realms with my own unique customizations. Nraanon most closely resembles a fantasy world, with Dwarves, Humans, Orcs, Elves, and other standard fantasy races making up the majority of the population. There are also Tieflings, Genasi, Minotaurs, and several other more exotic races. These races will be largely recognizable by most fans of fantasy, but most will also be unique to this setting in terms of quirks, physiology, and culture. The most notable shift will be the role that magic has to play in Nraanon.

A thousand years ago, a mysterious rupture in the planar fabric caused all magic to cease function for nearly ten minutes. This caused the utopian flying cities to crash down to the earth, leaving the millions dead and civilization in ruins. There were survivors of course, but they soon learned that life had changed forever. A new manner of creature began to roam the land, greedily feeding on magical energy wherever it could be found. The mages that did survive the Fall.
