Overview

The Elemental Plane of Magma is positioned to the South of the Great Wheel cosmology, and is also known as Vulken. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Creation and Debauchery, and is considered to represent the Chaotic Neutral alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Conjuration magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
