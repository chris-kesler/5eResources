Subclass: The Virtuoso

A paragon of invention and ingenuity, the Virtuoso has created and incorporated a truly sustainable energy source. This energy source grants the Virtuoso several at-will Technologies and capabilities, such as flight. The Virtuoso can also adapt other suits of Technological Armor to fit their needs.


Engineerism

Virtuoso: 2nd level

You gain one sustained Tech from the sustained Tech list. In addition, you can also convert any Technological Armor you have access to into your Visionary Armor as normal, retaining the original item's benefits in addition to the Visionary Armor features. You must still meet the level prerequisites for the Armor in order to do so, but ignore all other restrictions as you modify it to accomodate you. (Visionary Armor refers to Augmented Armor, Advanced Armor, Superior Armor, and Ultimate Armor simultaneously.)


Micronuclear

Virtuoso: 8th level

Subclass: The Glyphologist

A scholar of ancient knowledge and mythology, the Glyphologist combines modern science with ancient magical power sources. Whether it be inscribing runes, compressing motes of power, or integrating powerful artifacts, the Glyphologist binds themselves and their armor together on a supernatural level. While not a spellcaster, the Glyphologist still benefits from magical boons that manifest in a variety of ways. The Glyphologist can also adapt other suits of Magical Armor to fit their needs.
