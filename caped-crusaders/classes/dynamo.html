Subclass: The Militant

Honing their already immense power with training and discipline, the Militant balances their power with resourcefulness.


Techcasting

Militant: 3rd level

You have gained knowledge of advanced technology, and are capable of apply these technologies in a variety of ways. See the Rules for Techcasting mechanics and Technologies list.

  • Technologies Known: You learn Technologies of your choice as shown in the Consumables Known column of the class table. You may not learn a Technology of a level higher than your Max Consumable Level, and you may learn a Technology at the same time you learn its prerequisite.

  • Tech Points: You have a number of Tech Points as shown in the Consumable Points column of the class table. You expend these Tech Points to cast Technologies. You regain all expended Tech Points when you finish a long rest.

  • Max Tech Level: Many Technologies can be overpowered, consuming more Tech Points to create a greater effect. You can overpower these abilities to a maximum level, which increases at higher levels, as shown in the Max Consumable Level column of the class table. You may only cast Technologies at 6th, 7th, 8th, and 9th-level once. You regain the ability to do so after a long rest.

  • Techcasting Ability: Your Techcasting ability is Intelligence. You use your Intelligence modifier whenever a Technology refers to your Techcasting ability. Additionally, you use this ability score modifier when setting the saving throw DC for a Technology you cast and when making an attack roll with one.

  • Technology save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Techcasting ability modifier

  • Technology attack modifier = your proficiency bonus + your Techcasting ability modifier


Tech Weapon Application

Militant: 9th level

Attacks using Strength made with Technological Weapons now qualify for Mach Punch damage.


Tech Resilience

Militant: 17th level

You have advantage on all saving throws against Tech based effects. In addition, any Tech based attack roll (Techcasting, not regular attacks) has disadvantage to hit you.
