Overview

The Elemental Plane of Air is positioned at the top of the Great Wheel cosmology. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Knowledge and Freedom, and is widely considerd to represent the Chaotic Good alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Divination magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school. The Plane of Air is adjacent to the other Primal Planes of Water to the right and Fire to the left. The Border Planes at these junctions are the Plane of Ice and the Plane of Ash, which are also known as the Kingdom of Celestials and the Shadowfell respectively.
