Nraanon Wiki: Monsters

Overview

You may have noticed that the monster sections available on the previous page are somewhat different than those seen in 5th Edition, and that is intentional. I find that classifying Humanoids as monsters is irksome, so they have been removed and left to be covered elsewhere in the wiki. Specific species such as Aarakocra are covered under Races, and generic individuals with given abilities have been covered under Factions. Given the very intertwined planar structure of my world, I have decided to classify Oozes as elementals. This may seem strange since there are a wide variety of Oozes that other Elementals do not have, but given the accelerated evolutionary pace of the Plane of Ooze, I think the variation makes a fair amount of sense. On top of that, there are only so many ways a Fire Elemental can manifest, making the difference much more understandable.
