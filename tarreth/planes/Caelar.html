Overview

The Elemental Plane of Air is positioned to the Northeast of the Great Wheel cosmology. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Knowledge and Wrath, and is considered to represent the Neutral alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Illusion magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
