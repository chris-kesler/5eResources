Overview

The Elemental Plane of Fire is positioned to the Southeast of the Great Wheel cosmology. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Destruction and Consumption, and is considered to represent the Neutral alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Evocation magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
