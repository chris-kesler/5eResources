Overview

The Elemental Plane of Ice is positioned to the North of the Great Wheel cosmology, and is also known as Eifrost. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Knowledge and Order, and is considered to represent the Lawful Neutral alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Divination magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
