Myrmidos Wiki

Overview

The world of Myrmidos is a rather messy mashup of multiple genres. It includes a modern day Earth with a notable number of superheroes and supervillains. Within the history of Myrmidos, an era that draws heavily from the Forgotten Realms is long past. Magic used to reign supreme, which is where many of the world mythologies came from. However, mankind proved too immature as a species to wield magic, and so the knowledge of spellcasting was purged from Myrmidos by the Gehrdali, the oldest order of elves and original masters of magic.

Thousands of years later in the present, humans are creating their own sources of power through technology, and learning to wield it. This technology sometimes leads to accidents that create superpowered individuals, benevolent or otherwise. Rogue visitors from other worlds are increasing in frequency, possessing abilities inherent in their species that make them demigods on Myrmidos.

In light of the ever increasing rate of volatility, several members of the Gehrdali have decided to test human capacity for power by allowing a select few human mages to be trained. The advancement of technology, prominence of superpowers, and resurgence of magic, makes Myrmidos a chaotic and exciting place with an uncertain-but magnificent-future.
