Overview

The Elemental Plane of Earth is positioned to the Southwest of the Great Wheel cosmology. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Transformation and Stability, and is considered to represent the Neutral alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Transmutation magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
