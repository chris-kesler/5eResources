Overview

The Elemental Plane of Ash is positioned to the East of the Great Wheel cosmology, and is also known as the Shadowfell. It is strongly associated with domains relating to Death and Undeath, and is considered to represent the Neutral Evil alignment in its purest form. This plane is the source of all Necromancy magic, channeling raw energy from the Weave and converting it to magic of the aforementioned school.
