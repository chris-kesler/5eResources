Thermite Missile

Level Casting Time Range/Area Components
3rd 1 Action 120 feet (30 foot radius) S, M
Duration School Attack/Save Damage/Effect
Instananeous Munitions Dexterity Incinerating

Choose a point within 120 feet of you. Creatures and Vehicles within 30 feet must make a Dex save, taking 8d8 Incinerating damage on a failed save and half as much on a successful save. Vehicles that are made of conventional materials take double damage on a failed save, and normal damage on a successful save. Objects made of conventional materials within 30 feet of the point are destroyed. When you overcharge this Technology, you increase the base damage by 2d8 and increase the radius by 5 for each level beyond 3rd.
