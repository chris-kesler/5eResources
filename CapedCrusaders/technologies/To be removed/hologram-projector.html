Hologram Projector

Level Casting Time Range/Area Components
2nd 1 Action 30 feet (10 foot cube) V, S, M
Duration School Attack/Save Damage/Effect
Instananeous Optics Investigation Utility

You create a hologram within a ten foot cube whose center is no farther than 30 feet away from you. Anyone perceiving the hologram whose passive Investigation is less than your Techcasting DC belives it is real. A creature who inspects the hologram closely discerns its true nature upon a successful Investigation check against your Techcasting DC. The hologram is intangible and allows physical objects to freely pass through it.
