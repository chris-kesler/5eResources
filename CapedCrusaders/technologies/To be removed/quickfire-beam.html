Quickfire Beam

Level Casting Time Range/Area Components
Cantrip 1 Action 60 feet S,M
Duration School Attack/Save Damage/Effect
Instananeous Optics Ranged Energy

Make a ranged Tech attack against a number of targets equal to your proficiency bonus -1 of your choice within 60 feet. On a hit, a target suffers 1d6 Energy damage.
