Repulsing Blast

Level Casting Time Range/Area Components
Cantrip 1 Action 30 feet S, M
Duration School Attack/Save Damage/Effect
Instananeous Optics Ranged Concussive

Make a ranged Tech attack against one creature within 30 feet of you. On a hit, that creatures suffers Concussive damage equal to a number of d6s equal to your proficiency bonus -1 and is pushed 5 feet away from you.
