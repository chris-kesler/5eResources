Magnum Punch

Level Casting Time Range/Area Components
1st 1 Action 5 feet S, M
Duration School Attack/Save Damage/Effect
Instananeous Mechanical Engineering Melee Bludgeoning

Make a melee Tech attack against one creature of your choice within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 Bludgeoning damage and the next melee attack made against the target before the end of your next turn has advantage.
